Номінації

Цього року ми оголошуємо два типи номінацій — Professional nominations та Team nominations. Взяти участь у конкурсі можуть як IT фахівці особисто, так і компанії та команди з інноваційно-технологічними проектами, які мають вагомий вплив на розвиток галузі та суспільства.

Individual nominations Nominations

Категорія професійних персональних нагород IT Awards, яка відзначає найталановитіших IT спеціалістів України за їхні професійні заслуги.

applied applications
Подати заявку

In the scope of this nomination we are looking for brightest talents in the management field. No matter whether you are managing Team, Project, Program, Portfolio, Service or the whole organization, we evaluate only management practices and skills you've demonstrated.

Juri
Our key evaluation focuses are:

Constraints Management - tools and techniques to manage Scope, Schedule, Cost, Resources, Legal environment or any other limitation that you faced.

Strategy and Goals Management - methodologies and frameworks to set up long term vision, goals and targets for teams and individuals.

Life Cycle Management - choosing the right life cycle (e.g. Plan-Driven, Iterative, V-Model, Service Model, etc) and practices to manage specific stages (e.g. Planning, Execution, Closure, Design, Maintenance, etc.).

Value and Benefits Management - how you measure, evaluate and control performance, effectiveness and impact on the business.

Communication and Stakeholder Management. Governance.

Quality Management.

Human Resource/People Management.

Risk Management.

We are not looking for widely used practices (e.g. Scrum), rather emergent and innovative ways to achieve goals by application of the management toolset (e.g. Monte-Carlo risk analysis). In order to create an award-winning application, please share up to 3 cases from your own experience highlighting managing practices according to the structure above. Do not hesitate to share experience of fail or crisis management.

Please note that in scope of this nomination we are assessing personal achievements, not team or subordinate contribution. Any practices and approaches which were not implemented by associates, but by their subordinates will not be evaluated or assessed.
